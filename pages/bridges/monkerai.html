ENGINEERING DETAILS

Truss Type:
70' Bennett Truss (Old PWD)

Geometry: 3 truss spans, 3 approach spans, total length approximately 100 m

Strength: originally designed to carry pedestrians and cattle, now carries >40 tonne trucks
